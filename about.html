Our Story

From a small script to an enterprise ecosystem.

Founded with a rebellious spirit and a lofty objective: to offer enterprise-grade business tools without the enterprise-grade complexity.

Ninja Developer started as a simple internal CRM for a small agency. As our needs grew, so did the software. We realized that thousands of other businesses were facing the same fractured toolset we were.

Today, we serve thousands of users, processing millions in transactions, all from one unified dashboard.
